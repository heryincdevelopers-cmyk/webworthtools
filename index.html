FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the website value estimate?+
Our estimates use publicly available traffic ranking data and advertising revenue models. While not exact (only site owners have real analytics), they provide a reliable benchmark for comparison and tracking progress over time.
Is WebWorthTools completely free to use?+
Yes — all tools on WebWorthTools are 100% free with no registration or sign-up required. We keep the site running through non-intrusive advertising.
How is website worth calculated?+
We estimate worth based on: traffic data from public ranking systems, estimated advertising revenue using standard CPM/CPC rates, domain authority signals, and domain age. The result represents potential value if the site were monetized through ads and affiliate programs.
Can I check competitor websites?+
Absolutely. You can analyze any publicly accessible website, making WebWorthTools perfect for competitive research and benchmarking your site against others in your industry or niche.
What other tools are available on WebWorthTools?+
Beyond website valuation, we offer a currency converter (150+ currencies with live rates), BMI calculator, unit converter (length, weight, temperature, speed, area, volume), IP address lookup, word counter, password generator, percentage calculator, domain age checker, meta tag checker, color picker, and loan calculator — all completely free.
