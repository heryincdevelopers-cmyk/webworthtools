About This Unit Converter

Our free unit converter covers all essential measurement categories used worldwide. Whether you need to convert kilometers to miles for travel planning, kilograms to pounds for fitness tracking, or Celsius to Fahrenheit for cooking — get accurate results instantly with no registration required.

Simply select a measurement category using the tabs above, enter your value, choose the units, and your result appears immediately. Click the swap button to reverse the conversion direction in one click.

Supported Unit Categories

Length (meter, kilometer, mile, foot, inch, yard, nautical mile), Weight (kilogram, gram, pound, ounce, ton, stone), Temperature (Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin), Speed (km/h, mph, m/s, knots), Area (m², km², acre, hectare, square foot), and Volume (liter, gallon, milliliter, cup, fluid ounce, tablespoon).
