What Are Meta Tags?

Meta tags are HTML elements that provide information about a webpage to search engines and social media platforms. They appear in the <head> section of a webpage and are invisible to regular visitors, but crucial for SEO and social sharing.

Most Important Meta Tags for SEO

The title tag (50–60 characters) and meta description (150–160 characters) are the most critical. They determine how your page appears in Google search results. The Open Graph tags (og:title, og:description, og:image) control how your page looks when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp. Twitter Card tags do the same for Twitter/X.

How to Improve Your Meta Tags

Keep your title under 60 characters and include your primary keyword near the beginning. Write meta descriptions under 160 characters that clearly describe the page content and include a call to action. Make sure every page has a unique title and description — duplicate tags are a common SEO mistake.
