How Are Exchange Rates Determined?

Exchange rates fluctuate constantly based on global economic factors including interest rates, inflation, trade balances, and market sentiment. The rates shown are mid-market rates — the midpoint between buy and sell rates — which represent the fairest available benchmark. Banks and exchange services typically charge a margin above this rate.
